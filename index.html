welcome to m-tricks vet
formulations

M-Tricks Vet Formulations is a registered Private Limited Company in India under the Company Act, 1956. The company is a leading animal healthcare products manufacturer in India and is FAMI QS, ISO 22000:2018, and HACCP certified. M-Tricks Vet Formulations deals in the production and supply of veterinary pharmaceutical products to promote animal health and welfare. The company is also engaged in the manufacturing and export of animal feed supplements, to enhance the quality and nutritional value of animal feed. With a team of qualified professionals and state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities, M-Tricks Vet Formulations is committed to providing innovative and reliable solutions for animal healthcare.
